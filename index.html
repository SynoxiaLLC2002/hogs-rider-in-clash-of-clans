Best Clash of Clans Troop Combinations for Every Town Hall Level

In Clash of Clans, your troops are the heart of your offensive strategy. Choosing the right combination for each Town Hall level can make the difference between victory and defeat, especially when attacking higher-level bases or defending your own. This article covers some of the most effective troop combinations for different Town Hall levels to help you maximize your attacking potential.

Town Hall 7: Classic Giant-Healer Combo

For players at Town Hall 7, the Giant-Healer combination is an effective and affordable strategy. Here’s how it works:

  • Giants serve as tanky units to absorb damage from defenses.
  • Healers follow the Giants, keeping them alive longer.
  • Archers and Wizards clean up remaining buildings.

This combo works well against bases with centralized air defenses. However, be cautious if the base has well-protected air defenses, as they can quickly take down your Healers.

Town Hall 9: GoHo (Golem and Hog Rider) Strategy

At Town Hall 9, the GoHo attack strategy is highly popular. This combination of Golems and Hog Riders is effective for high-damage attacks. The method involves:

  • Golems to tank damage and draw fire.
  • Hog Riders to target defensive structures and clear the path.

Using Healing Spells with this combo is crucial, as it helps Hog Riders withstand damage from splash defenses like Wizard Towers and Bomb Towers. GoHo is ideal for bases with exposed air defenses and centralized resources.

Town Hall 10: Lavaloon (Lava Hound and Balloon) Attack

The Lavaloon strategy is a powerful air-based attack for Town Hall 10 and above. It combines Lava Hounds and Balloons to take out defenses from above. The strategy is as follows:

  • Deploy Lava Hounds to target air defenses and soak up damage.
  • Follow with Balloons to destroy defenses once the air defenses are occupied.

Support this attack with Haste Spells and Rage Spells to speed up the Balloons, ensuring they reach defenses quickly. This strategy requires precise timing and placement to work effectively.

Town Hall 12+: Hybrid Attack (Hog Riders and Miners)

The Hybrid Attack strategy is popular for Town Hall 12 and higher. Combining Hog Riders and Miners, this strategy can take down bases with spread-out defenses. Here’s the breakdown:

  • Start with a Queen Walk to clear out one side of the base.
  • Deploy Hog Riders and Miners on the opposite side to converge on the defenses.

Using Heal Spells in conjunction with this strategy keeps your troops alive as they move across the base. This approach works well against bases with both ground and air defenses, and it’s highly adaptable.

Conclusion

Mastering troop combinations at each Town Hall level will give you an advantage, whether you’re participating in clan wars, farming for resources, or pushing trophies. Experiment with these strategies to find the best fit for your play style. With the right troops and spells, you can effectively conquer even the toughest bases in Clash of Clans.
